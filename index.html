About BlocX

Built on Craft
& Precision

BlocX Interior Design is a Dubai-based studio specialising in high-performance commercial, retail, and residential interiors — at the intersection of strategic space planning and material excellence.

From 989 m² corporate headquarters to intimate boutique studios, every project is approached with the same rigour: concept development, technical coordination, regulatory compliance, and uncompromising site oversight.
